Frequently Asked Questions

Why to have a website?

A website is your online presence, your digital business card. It's where you showcase your brand, products, and services. It's where you connect with your clients and grow your business. Even if you have one, it could be outdated or not optimized for search engines or AI searches. It's important to have a website that is up to date and designed for modern internet.

How much does a custom website cost?

Website costs vary based on complexity and features. Simple business websites start around $1,500-$2,000, while more complex websites typically range from $5,000-$20,000. I provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs and goals.

Is there a tax benefit for having a website?

Absolutely! If you're a business owner, you can deduct the cost of your website from your taxes as a business expense. Check with your accountant as cases may vary.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects take 4-8 weeks from start to launch. Simple websites can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while complex or custom applications may take 8-12 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

What's involved in the website making process?

The process typically includes initial consultation, design, development, testing, domain and hosting setup, and launch.

Do you work with clients outside San Francisco?

Yes! While I'm based in the San Francisco Bay Area and prefer local clients for in-person meetings, I work with clients worldwide through video calls, project management tools, and regular communication.

What technologies do you use for web development?

I use modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and various CMS platforms. I focus on responsive design, fast loading times, and SEO and AI searchoptimization to ensure your website performs well across all devices.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages including security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and technical support. This ensures your website stays secure, fast, and up-to-date.

Can you help with SEO and online marketing?

Yes! I build websites with SEO best practices and can help with basic search engine optimization and online marketing strategies. Although I don't offer full-service marketing, I can refer you to trusted marketing professionals if needed.
